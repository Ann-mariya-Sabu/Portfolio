ART EVENT MANAGEMENT

Art Event Mangement is a comprehensive ticket booking website for art events, specifically designed for exhibitions, gallery shows, and other cultural showcases. The platform provides a smooth and engaging experience for art enthusiasts to explore upcoming events, view event details, and book tickets online. It features an intuitive user interface where visitors can filter events by type, location, or date, and receive digital tickets with QR codes for easy check-in at venues. Organizers are given access to a dedicated admin panel to manage event listings, monitor ticket sales, and communicate with attendees.

EVENTWIBE

EventWibe is a feature-rich and user-friendly ticket booking website designed for events such as concerts, music shows, and live performances. The platform allows users to easily browse upcoming events, book tickets securely, and manage their bookings through a seamless and intuitive interface. EventWibe offers functionalities like event discovery with filters, secure online payments, instant e-ticket generation with QR codes, and a personalized user dashboard to view booking history. It also includes an admin panel where event organizers can create and manage events, monitor ticket sales, and access attendee data.

DaVinci Resolve

DaVinci Resolve is a powerful and professional video editing software developed by Blackmagic Design. It is widely used in the film, television, and content creation industries due to its comprehensive suite of tools for video editing, color correction, visual effects (VFX), motion graphics, and audio post-production—all integrated into a single application. One of its standout features is its industry-leading color grading tools, which are trusted by Hollywood professionals. The software also includes Fusion for advanced visual effects and motion graphics, and Fairlight for complete audio editing and mixing. DaVinci Resolve supports high-resolution workflows up to 8K and HDR, and it is compatible with Windows, macOS, and Linux platforms.
